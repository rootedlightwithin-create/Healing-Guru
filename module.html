Find a comfortable position. Close your eyes if that feels safe to you.

{% elif 'Grounding' in tool_name %}
Let's ground together. Start by feeling your feet on the floor.
Press your feet down gently. Notice the support beneath you.
Now take a slow breath in through your nose... and out through your mouth.
Two more breaths like that. In... and out.
Notice the weight of your body. Feel how gravity holds you. You're supported right now.

You're here. You're present. You're okay.
{% elif 'Butterfly Hug' in tool_name %}
Cross your arms over your chest, hands resting on opposite shoulders.
Now gently tap your left shoulder with your right hand.
Then tap your right shoulder with your left hand.
Keep alternating. Left... right... left... right. Like a slow heartbeat.
Breathe slowly as you tap. Notice what you feel.
Continue for another 20 seconds or so. You're regulating your nervous system.
Gently stop. Notice if anything shifted. You might feel calmer, heavier, or more present.
{% elif 'Box Breathing' in tool_name %}
We're going to breathe together. Place one hand on your chest.
Breathe in slowly... 1, 2, 3, 4.
Hold your breath... 1, 2, 3, 4.
Breathe out slowly... 1, 2, 3, 4.
Hold again... 1, 2, 3, 4.
One more round. Breathe in... 1, 2, 3, 4.
Hold... 1, 2, 3, 4.
Breathe out... 1, 2, 3, 4. Let your shoulders drop, your jaw soften.
You're telling your body it's safe to slow down.

If you can, reach out and touch one of these things. Feel its texture.
Right now, in this moment, you are okay.
