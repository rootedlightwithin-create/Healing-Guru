Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under GDPR and other privacy laws.

1. Who We Are

Valiant Growth is a healing and personal growth platform designed to support your emotional well-being journey. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care and transparency.

Data Controller: Valiant Growth
Contact Email: privacy@valiantgrowth.com
Website: https://valiant-growth-production-3977.up.railway.app

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

  • Session ID: A unique anonymous identifier stored in your browser to maintain your session
  • IP Address: For security and consent verification purposes only
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent (anonymized analytics)

2.2 Data You Provide

  • Chat Messages: Your conversations with the AI healing guide
  • Journal Entries: Emotional check-ins and reflections you choose to save
  • Module Progress: Which healing modules you've started and completed
  • Community Posts & Comments: Content you share in the Community Circle (with optional display name or anonymous)
  • Subscription Information: Payment processed via Gumroad (we only store license key, not payment details)

2.3 Special Category Data (Sensitive Data)

Important: Under GDPR Article 9, data concerning health (including mental health and emotional well-being) is considered "special category" data requiring explicit consent and extra protection.

We process health-related data you provide through:

  • Chat conversations about emotional struggles
  • Journal entries describing feelings and mental states
  • Module reflections on healing journey

Legal Basis: We process this sensitive data based on your explicit consent (GDPR Article 9(2)(a)) and for the purpose of providing health services you've requested (GDPR Article 9(2)(h)).

3. Why We Collect This Data

Legitimate purposes under GDPR Article 6:

  • Service Delivery (6(1)(b)): To provide the healing modules, chat support, and community features you've requested
  • Your Consent (6(1)(a)): For optional features like analytics and personalized insights
  • Legal Obligation (6(1)(c)): To comply with legal requirements
  • Legitimate Interest (6(1)(f)): To improve our services, detect issues, and ensure platform security

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver healing modules and AI chat support tailored to your needs
  • Track your progress through healing paths
  • Enable community features (posts, comments)
  • Verify subscription access to premium content
  • Improve our platform and content based on usage patterns
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Send important updates about service changes (we don't send marketing emails)

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your data. We only share data when necessary:

  • Gumroad (Payment Processor): Handles subscription payments. See their privacy policy at gumroad.com/privacy
  • Railway (Hosting Provider): Hosts our database and application securely
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law or to protect safety

No third-party access to: Your chat messages, journal entries, or community posts remain private to you and our platform.

6. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

✅ Right to Access (Article 15)

Request a copy of all data we hold about you

✅ Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data

✅ Right to Erasure / "Right to Be Forgotten" (Article 17)

Request deletion of all your personal data

✅ Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Download your data in a machine-readable format (JSON)

✅ Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

Limit how we use your data

✅ Right to Object (Article 21)

Object to processing based on legitimate interest

✅ Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

Withdraw consent at any time (doesn't affect prior processing)

Exercise Your Rights:

  • Go to your Account Dashboard
  • Or email: privacy@valiantgrowth.com
  • We will respond within 30 days

7. Data Retention

How long we keep your data:

  • Active accounts: Data retained while you use the service
  • Inactive accounts: Session data auto-deleted after 180 days of inactivity
  • After account deletion: All personal data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., financial records for 7 years)
  • Anonymized data: Non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for service improvement

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • Encrypted database connections
  • Secure HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls limiting who can view data
  • Pseudonymization (session IDs instead of real names)

Data Breach Protocol: In the unlikely event of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33.

9. Cookies & Tracking

Essential Cookies (no consent required):

  • Session cookie: Maintains your login and progress (deleted when you close browser)

Non-Essential Cookies (requires consent):

  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the platform (if you accept cookies)

You can change cookie preferences anytime in your Account Dashboard or by clearing browser cookies.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in:

  • United States (Railway hosting)
  • European Union (if applicable based on hosting configuration)

If data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we ensure adequate safeguards through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Hosting providers with GDPR compliance certifications

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately at privacy@valiantgrowth.com.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on the platform.

13. Your Right to Complain

  • EU: Find your DPA at edpb.europa.eu
