## üì± What You Need to Add

### App Icons (Required)
Create two PNG icons and save them in the `static/` folder:

1. **icon-192.png** - 192x192 pixels
2. **icon-512.png** - 512x512 pixels

**Icon Design Tips:**
- Use your app logo/branding
- Simple, recognizable design
- High contrast colors
- No text (hard to read at small sizes)
- Square with rounded corners works best

**Free Icon Tools:**
- Canva (canva.com) - Use "Logo" template, export as PNG
- Figma (figma.com) - Free design tool
- Or use AI: "Create a purple gradient app icon with a lotus flower for a mental health app"

### Optional: Screenshot
- **screenshot.png** - 1080x1920 pixels (for app stores/listings)
- Take a screenshot of your app in action

## üöÄ How Users Install It

### On Android (Chrome):
1. Visit your website
2. Chrome shows "Install Healing Guru" banner at bottom
3. Tap "Install" ‚Üí app added to home screen
4. Opens like a native app (no browser bars)

### On iPhone (Safari):
1. Visit your website in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down ‚Üí "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" ‚Üí icon appears on home screen
